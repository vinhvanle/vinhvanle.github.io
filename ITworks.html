IT Works

This section is my summary of IT professional work from online sources

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WGN4Y-fw2PM

IT professionals usually work for vendors that want to upgrade or install their system. Depending on what company they work for, they will specialize in a specific environment. They will need to satisfy the need of their vendors and adjust, upgrade or install any new technologies or hardware needed. IT professionals would spend most of their time in the customers’ facility installing or upgrading systems. The most difficult aspect of IT professionals’ work is probably satisfying their customer’s demands. Sometimes, the customer doesn’t have the complete knowledge of the IT profession, they only have bits and pieces of information. And because of those bits and pieces of information, the customers will sometimes have unrealistic demands or doubts about the work of IT professionals. That would create frustration, and time consuming since the IT professionals have to explain or persuade the customers to trust and use the environment that the IT professionals are specialized in.

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=b7VVaIiaeKY

There are other IT works such as IT professionals that work on the IT helpdesk. The main job for IT helpdesk is to help fix any problems that pop up during the use of an environment that is installed in the system. If there’s any problem for users while using their devices, IT helpdesk professionals will come to where the problem is to help fix it and put everything back on track. Moreover, IT helpdesk professionals will help anyone that comes to the helpdesk to troubleshoot any problems that appear in their devices. IT helpdesk professionals will spend most of their time staying in their office. They have to be available at anytime during working hours to help people. There will be a lot of people that would need the help of IT professionals because they don’t have the full knowledge of their devices. There are various types of problems that could appear, most common would be passwords forgets or not being able to connect to the internet. There might sometimes that customers would have to questions regarding the possibility of their ideas or projects The most difficult aspect of their work would be helping someone that doesn’t have the slightest knowledge of technology. IT professionals will need to find the easiest way to help them understand how to operate the devices.

https://www.comptia.org/blog/a-day-in-the-life-of-an-it-pro-it-manager

IT professionals are needed virtually everywhere in the world and in any kind of organization, even in trucking. IT manager Janathan Dalia that works for Langer Transport Corporation shared his work in the article: “A Day in the Life of an IT Pro: IT Manager”. His job is to manage the voice, video and data connections at his company’s locations. He would be responsible to install, maintain, and upgrade the systems. He will also make sure their backups, server hardware and also their shutdown or emergency recovery system works perfectly well everyday. Most importantly, he needs to constantly write reports on their company’s data and also find ways to make their system better by decreasing costs or adding new technologies. In his typical day, he would start by looking at any problems or issues that are reported. After resolving those critical problems, he would spend time fixing the non-critical ones. Once all the problems have been resolved, he will spend time on his ongoing projects or upgrades at the facilities. His biggest issue while working as an IT manager would be managing different facilities at once since his company has multiple server locations and also multiple facilities.

https://swiftsystems.com/guides-tips/a-typical-day-in-the-life-of-your-company-it-guy-or-gal/

In the article “A Typical Day in the Life of Your Company IT Guy (or Gal)”, the author shared the time table of a typical IT professional. An IT professional would start their day by responding to any urgent needs or critical problems reported. This could range from huge problems such as virus in the system to the printer being jammed. People in the company will have all kinds of problems. Depending on the problem, the IT professional would need to have a physical look at the system or just by guiding the employees through online means. Depending on the size of the company, the amount of work that an IT professional has to do will vary. However, it is certainly not an easy task.

If an IT professional is working in the field of IT project manager, they will have to manage their teams and coordinate team members to do their tasks in a well-planned time table in order to finish the project on time. IT project manager would want to get the minor problems out of the way first thing in the morning so that he or she can keep a good flow of their productivity of the day. IT project manager would also needs to have meeting with the VPs to constantly update and acquire the VPs’ demands on the project. Project manager also needs to have meetings with the teams that are developing the project to get updates on the progress of the project. During the course of the day, project manger will also need to have meeting with their customers to give updates and discuss any new demands. Moreover, project manager will also need to constantly update their knowledge on new and existing technologies to strengthen their ability in consulting customers and also the developer teams. The most challenging aspect of their work would probably be the commuting process and also managing different projects at the same time.
